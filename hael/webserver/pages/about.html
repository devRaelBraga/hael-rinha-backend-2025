About Monkey Language

Monkey Language is a lightweight and intuitive programming language built with Go, designed to make coding accessible, fun, and powerful. Created in 2025, it aims to bridge the gap between simplicity and functionality for developers of all levels.

Our Mission

To empower developers with a language that is easy to learn yet capable of building real-world applications.

Open Source

Monkey Language is open source, inviting contributions from developers worldwide to shape its future.

Join our community and help us build the next generation of programming tools!
